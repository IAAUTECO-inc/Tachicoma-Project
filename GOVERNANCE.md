# Project Tachikoma Governance

## Model: Benevolent Dictatorship & Sovereign Oversight
Project Tachikoma is lead by **IA_AUT_ECO**. Decisions regarding the architectural core and security primitives are made by the Core Maintainers to ensure strict alignment with the Sovereign AI Manifesto.

## Decision Making
1. **Architectural Integrity:** Changes to the kernel-interface or AEGIS-compliance layers require unanimous approval from the lead architects.
2. **Public Interest:** Contributions are evaluated based on their impact on digital sovereignty and inclusion for people with disabilities.
